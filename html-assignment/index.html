About me

My name is Sophia Rodriguez-Kellett . I was born and raised in Ottawa with my parents and my 3 siblings, I was born on July 22nd 2004. I grew up visiting my family from Panama, as my mother was born there, so I've spent a lot of time experiencing both sides of my culture. I love to travel and see the world, and I hope to one day visit some of the top places on my list!

I have a passion for the arts and all things technology. I am currently taking Interactive media design at Algonquin college to further my knowledge on the things that I really enjoy. In the future I hope to be the CEO of my own business and work remotely, as the idea of being my own boss really motivates me to be good at what I am doing.
