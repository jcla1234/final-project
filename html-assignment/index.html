About me -

My name is Sophia Rodriguez-Kellett, born and raised in Ottawa with my parents and three siblings. My birthday is July 22nd, 2004. Growing up, I had the privilege of experiencing both sides of my cultural heritage by visiting my family in Panama, where my mother was born. These experiences have shaped my outlook on life. I have passion for travel and a desire to explore the world. I've made a list of top destinations I hope to visit one day. I have an interest for the arts and technology. I chose to study Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College, this path allows me to further my knowledge in areas I'm truly passionate about. In the future, I aspire to be the CEO of my own business, working remotely. The idea of being my own boss serves as a powerful motivator, driving me to excel in everything I do.
